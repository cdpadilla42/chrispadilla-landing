About Me

Hey there, I’m Chris! My first foray into web development began when I was a kid, thanks to a little website called Neopets. I was enchanted by the unique layouts and themes users were crafting for their pages.

I was inspired to do the same and soon enough, after learning, experimenting, and plain old having fun, I was building profiles, shops, and full blown website layouts for my friends and I with the basics of HTML and CSS.

While pursuing a creative career as a music performer and teacher, I’ve kept returning to building sites for myself and other musicians. To me, this has always been the most engaging part of any creative project! With this realization, I was driven to fully dive into learning development tools and design.

Today I’ve maintained a childlike wonder and curiosity with building on the web. I’ve since updated my skills for modern web development using prominent industry tools. One thing that remains the same, however, is my desire to creatively solve problems through code while designing pleasant and inclusive experiences on the web for all.
